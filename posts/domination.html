Domination


Domination is a minimal & unique multi-player strategy game in which you have to dominate the board by converting all the tiles to your color.

Rules:

Start the path from tile of your color and end the path at a tile (which was already) of your color, whilst converting the opponent's tiles. The player who dominates the board by converting all the tiles to his/her color, wins!

Features:

- Single Player Mode:
Play vs Computer with three levels of AI: Novice, Semi-Pro & Pro.
Leaderboards: 5 Points for Novice, 50 for Semi-Pro & 500 for Pro.
Matches Manager: Maintains the matches details.

- Local Multiplayer Mode:
Play with your friend locally, i.e. on the same device.

Have fun! :)
